# Market data

`@orderly.network/hooks` provides the following hooks related to market data:

- [`useMarketsStream`](/apis/modules/__orderly_network___hooks/useMarketsStream) - Retreive all listed symbols and its market data
- [`useMarkPrice`](/apis/modules/__orderly_network___hooks/useMarkPrice) - Get the mark price of a single symbol
- [`useMarkPricesStream`](/apis/modules/__orderly_network___hooks/useMarkPricesStream) - Get the mark price of all symbols
- [`useMarketTradeStream`](/apis/modules/__orderly_network___hooks/useMarketTradeStream) - Get the market trades history of a symbol
